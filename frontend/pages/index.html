Everything you need

Built for serious businesses

Every feature your team needs to track time accurately, manage breaks and understand productivity.

Real-Time Timer

Start, pause, resume and stop sessions with a single click. Live timer syncs across devices instantly.

Break Management

Track tea breaks, lunch, toilet breaks and meetings separately — each with its own dedicated timer.

Session Approvals

Admins review, approve or reject completed sessions with notes. Full audit trail for every hour logged.

Multi-Company

Each company gets a unique Admin ID. Staff join with that ID. Full isolation between companies.

Export Reports

One-click export to PDF or Excel. Beautiful formatted reports ready for payroll, HR or audits.

Events on Shift

Real-time group chat for your team during shifts. Share updates, announcements and stay connected.
