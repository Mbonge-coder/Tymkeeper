How we got here

From frustration to product

It started in a small Johannesburg office where the business owner was spending every Friday afternoon manually reconciling work hours from WhatsApp messages, handwritten notes and blurry photos of sign-in sheets.

The big HR platforms were either too expensive, too complicated, or built for multinational corporations — not for a 12-person team running a logistics business in Boksburg.

So we built the thing we wished existed. Something clean enough for staff who aren't tech-savvy, powerful enough for admin who need real data, and priced honestly enough for businesses that are still growing.

TymKeeper is the result of listening to dozens of South African business owners and building exactly what they needed — nothing more, nothing less.
